Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is more than ruins—it's where Sri Lanka's heart still beats strong. Walk in the footsteps of ancient kings, meditate under the world's oldest tree, and witness living traditions that have endured for millennia. This sacred city doesn't just show you history—it lets you feel it in every stone, every prayer, and every sunset over ancient stupas.

Discover Sri Lanka's First Kingdom & Sacred Buddhist Capital

Step into a UNESCO World Heritage Site that whispers tales from over 2,500 years ago. Anuradhapura isn't just an archaeological site—it's a living pilgrimage center where ancient stupas rise majestically, sacred Bodhi trees grow, and the spiritual heartbeat of Sri Lanka continues to thrive. As the island's first capital and one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities, this sacred city offers an awe-inspiring journey through time, faith, and architectural brilliance.

Why Anuradhapura Should Be on Your Sri Lanka Itinerary

Experience Living Spiritual Heritage
Anuradhapura remains an active pilgrimage site where devotees in white offer flowers and prayers amidst awe-inspiring ancient structures. Feel the palpable spiritual energy that has drawn pilgrims for millennia.

Other Attractions

  • Nine Arch Bridge: Also called the Bridge in the Sky, a stunning colonial-era viaduct built with a challenging nine-degree curve and steep gradient.
  • Peacock: Often sighted freely roaming near the countryside and sacred areas, adding to the charm of the region.

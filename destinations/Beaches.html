Sri Lanka’s Beaches

Diverse coasts, golden sands & world-class surf — year-round paradise

Sri Lanka's coastline is astonishingly diverse, offering a perfect beach for every type of traveler. From palm-fringed golden sands to world-class surf breaks and vibrant coral reefs, the island is a year-round beach destination.

South & West Coasts (November – April)

Bentota & Beruwala: Calm beaches ideal for families and water sports.

Hikkaduwa: Coral reefs, snorkeling, and nightlife.

Mirissa & Weligama: Whale watching and beginner-friendly surf beaches.

Unawatuna: Sheltered swimming bays and coastal cafés.

East Coast (May – September)

Nilaveli & Uppuveli: White sands and turquoise waters near Trincomalee.

Arugam Bay: One of the world’s top surf destinations with a relaxed surf culture.

Ancient Kingdoms of Sri Lanka

2,500+ years of royal history, architecture, and civilization

Anuradhapura Kingdom

Founded in the 4th century BCE, Anuradhapura served as the first great capital of Sri Lanka. The city is famous for its massive stupas, advanced irrigation systems, and sacred Bodhi Tree.

Polonnaruwa Kingdom

Polonnaruwa rose to prominence in the 11th century, showcasing refined stone architecture, royal palaces, and Buddhist sculptures.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Built by King Kashyapa, Sigiriya is a masterpiece of urban planning, combining royal gardens, frescoes, and a dramatic rock citadel.

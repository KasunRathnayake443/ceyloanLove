Discover the hidden gem of Ella and the gateway to Sri Lanka’s highlands in Nanu Oya on a guided tour. Explore the serene hill station of Ella, known for its stunning natural beauty, cool climate, and laid-back charm. Then, visit the charming town of Nanu Oya, a key entry point to the country’s picturesque hill country.

Start your day in Ella, a serene hill station known for its stunning natural beauty, cool climate, and laid-back charm. Surrounded by emerald tea plantations, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls, Ella is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Highlights include the iconic Nine Arches Bridge, Little Adam’s Peak, and the scenic train ride through the highlands. Whether hiking through verdant trails, enjoying panoramic views, or savoring the local cuisine, Ella offers a tranquil escape with unforgettable experiences.

Next, head to Nanu Oya, a charming town in Sri Lanka's central province, which serves as a key entry point to the country’s picturesque hill country. Surrounded by lush tea plantations, rolling hills, and cool mountain air, it is the railway stop for the nearby town of Nuwara Eliya, also known as "Little England."

Travelers often delight in the scenic train ride that passes through Nanu Oya, offering breathtaking views of waterfalls, misty mountains, and vibrant greenery. Whether you’re heading to Nuwara Eliya or exploring the tranquil surroundings, Nanu Oya is a serene and scenic stop worth experiencing.
